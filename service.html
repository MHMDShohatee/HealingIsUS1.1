Our Services

Welcome to the "Healing is US " information page. We are the premier provider of Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation. Our goal is to provide the very best care in spine and joint care, acute and chronic pain, functional limitations, cardiac and pulmonary rehabilitation, pelvic floor rehabilitation, geriatric and pediatric care, mobility restoration, and posture correction. Because our quality control standards are high, our Physical Therapists are among the best in the business. Our goal is to always deliver the best individualized care possible. We want you, our valued customer, to be happy. Here is a general list of our services.

Manual Physical Therapy

The clinic's manual physical therapy services focus on hands-on techniques to evaluate and treat musculoskeletal conditions. This includes joint mobilization, soft tissue massage, and other manual interventions to reduce pain, improve range of motion, and restore function.

Rehabilitation

The rehabilitation services at the clinic aim to help patients recover from injuries, illnesses, or surgical procedures. This can involve therapeutic exercises, balance training, gait training, and other modalities tailored to the individual's needs and goals.

Medical Massage

The clinic offers specialized medical massage techniques. This can include deep tissue massage, trigger point therapy, and myofascial release to address specific muscle imbalances or areas of tension.

Cupping Therapy

The clinic provides cupping treatment as a complementary therapy to help reduce muscle pain and improve circulation. Dry and wet cupping are available

Individualized Exercise Plan

For each patient, the physical therapists develop a personalized exercise program to address their specific impairments and functional limitations. This may involve strengthening, flexibility, balance, and cardiovascular components.

Gym Availability

The clinic has a gym on-site with various exercise equipment. Patients can use the gym facilities as part of their supervised therapy sessions or for independent workouts under the guidance of the physical therapy staff
